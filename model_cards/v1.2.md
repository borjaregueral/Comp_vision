## Intended use
Detect, segment and classify vehicle damage in parking-claim photos, as the
*perception* stage of `assess_claim.py`. The model supplies damage candidates only;
all operational decisions (cost, severity, triage lane) are made by deterministic
downstream rules. **Not** intended to auto-resolve claims on its own.

## Classes (6)
`scratch, dent, crack, paint_chip, puncture, broken_light`

Created by re-typing existing crops (no new photos): `auto_relabel.py` runs CLIP
zero-shot classification **constrained by the original coarse label**, and recovers
**20,082** previously-discarded mono-class Roboflow boxes. See `configs/taxonomy_v2.yaml`.

## Training data — `data/final_v2`
- **39,724 annotations** over **17,498 images**, splits **12,246 / 3,497 / 1,755** (train/val/test).
- Sources: CarDD, VehiDE, Roboflow (recovered).
- **Confidence floor 0.55** applied (`unify_to_yolo --min-conf`): low-confidence auto-labels
  and now-empty images dropped (no false-background signal).
- Class balance (annotations): scratch 16,644 · dent 8,145 · crack 6,097 ·
  broken_light 4,731 · paint_chip 2,266 · puncture 1,841. **paint_chip & puncture are minority classes.**

## Training
2-phase from COCO-pretrained `yolo11m-seg.pt`, imgsz 1024, AdamW.
Phase 1: 20 ep, backbone frozen (AMP). Phase 2: `--no-amp`, batch 4, stopped at
**epoch 65** (val mAP plateaued ~7 epochs). **`--no-amp` is mandatory in phase 2** —
AMP diverges `seg_loss` to NaN at unfreeze.

## Known biases & limitations
- **paint_chip is unreliable** (recall 0.15 — misses ~85%). Cause: rarest class +
  noisiest auto-labels. Recommend merging it away or sourcing real paint-chip labels.
- **Recall is modest** on the core classes (~0.37–0.44). For an insurance use case a
  false negative = underpayment, so production needs a lower operating threshold and/or
  more in-domain data.
- **Domain gap**: trained on public web/accident datasets, **not** real garage phone
  photos. Test metrics likely overstate production performance.
- **Labels are CLIP auto-generated** with sampled human spot-check, not fully verified.
- **Bias audit by make/colour/province (T5.2) NOT performed** — the public training
  datasets carry no such vehicle metadata. Deferred to the golden-set evaluation, which does.
- **Downstream economics are PLACEHOLDER**; **parts/zone model not wired** (T4.5).
